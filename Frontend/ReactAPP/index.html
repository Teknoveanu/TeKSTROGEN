The Underground Beat Revolution

TeKSTROGEN is more than just a radio station - it's the lifeblood of the underground electronic music scene. Born from the spirit of free parties and sound system culture, we represent the raw, unfiltered essence of tekno in all its forms.

Our mission is to preserve and promote the authentic underground experience, connecting communities worldwide through the universal language of beats.

From acid tekno to hardtek, from forest raves to warehouse parties, TeKSTROGEN pulses through the veins of the global free party movement.
