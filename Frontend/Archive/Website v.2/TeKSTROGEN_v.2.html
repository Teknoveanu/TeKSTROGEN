About TeKSTROGEN

TeKSTROGEN emerged from the underground free party scene in 2020, born from a collective of artists and DJs dedicated to preserving the authentic spirit of tekno culture.


Our mission is to provide a platform for underground music that exists outside the mainstream, governed by principles of DIY ethics, mutual aid, and creative freedom.


We believe in music as a form of resistance - creating temporary autonomous zones where conventional rules are suspended and new possibilities can emerge.


Our radio station broadcasts 24/7, featuring live sets from events, studio sessions from emerging artists, and curated programs exploring the many facets of underground electronic music.
